Welcome to Manifest Life.

Manifest Life is a nonprofit lifestyle brand helping people self-identify as leaders, providing financial and consulting help. The brand believes in fostering an ecosystem that is built upon design-thinking and solution-based collaboration. The brand specifically targets the Millennial generation. Manifest also received a Small Business Support Grant and participated in the Small Business Incubator Program at Lasell College.
